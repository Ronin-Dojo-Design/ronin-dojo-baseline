# Empty Hand Waza

- basic punches and kicks
  - jab, cross, uppercut, hook, lead-side, oblique, side, front, round house
  - single wide punch
    - double block
    - double block targeting the shoulder
    - double block with invisible punch
    - double block with transition to under hook the upper arm
    - double block with attack to face (close hand blocks, far hand attacks)
    - double block, head chop, knee to body
      - kick knee
    - osioto gari (throw to their back)
      - under hook and pull (throw to their front)
- single straight punch
  - parry and hit (to face, to body, destructions)
  - gunting entrances
    - (open) close hand parry to dead side, far hand bicep destruction
    - (open) far hand parry to live side, close hand tricep destruction (likely causing hyper extension of elbow)
    - a L lock
      - (closed) parry far hand to dead side, close dumog to elbow into head and arm throw
    - chicken wing 2
      - old school entangled arm
      - arm bar with upper cut reference
        - entangled arm from low punch
        - wing tai from high punch (parry towards their head)
        - #2 lock from high punch (parry around your arm)
- jab cross defense
  - parry parry from kick
  - parry parry from su
  - parry jab down, block cross high, enter into front clinch
  - entangled arm flow against 2 punches, jab cross
    - 2 count: parry and hold, ton sau, punch comes and is parried with far hand
    - 3 count dead side: parry, check, uppercut, punch is parried with uppercut hand
    - 3 count live side low: parry, check, hit face, low punch is wrapped with striking hand and secured at elbow 
      with check hand, second punch is parried with check hand into entangled arm
    - 3 count live side high: parry, check, hit face, high punch is blocked with striking hand as check hand 
      comes in to grab wrist, striking hand dumog on the elbow into head and arm throw
    - inside/outside lock flow
    - dumog flow
  - flows on 1-2
  - 6 count subayan (simultaneous parry/hit or block/hit)
  - abecedario 8

Flow Drills
- kawat pull (gain sensitivity, add counters: ridge hand to face, hit to body, hit to face, entrance into locks)
- windshield wiper parry drill
