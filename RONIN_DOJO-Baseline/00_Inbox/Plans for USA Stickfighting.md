Plans for the Doce Pares Family  Invitational League:

Mission statement:

We invite all and welcome all. 
No politics, no ego, just stick fighting run by enthusiasts and World Champions who love the sport. Our approach is collective and organized, with no pretense or politics involved. Our goal is to run a tournament that brings people together.

Run by stick fighters who stick fight who want to get together with stick fighters who want to stick fight, and stick fight.  

For those who love stick fighting.

Run in the spirit of Diony Cañete and Dong Cuesta, in the spirit of the Old WEKAF, with professionally run, fun community, and fierce competition performed with admiration, intensity, integrity, and respect.

Join us in working towards a USA Representative team for the Doce Pares World Championships, where the top 20-30 (or other agreed upon setup) competitors from each year of sanctioned tournaments across the country  will earn a higher seeding (or something else agreed upon) at Nationals for an end of year event, anyone can compete at Nationals and is invited to compete at National event regardless, with similar or other agreed upon invite to the Doce Pares World Championships. Regardless of division or seating placements, the top 50 will be selected based on their performance.

Or just invite everyone that competed at one event, something simple for a bit.  

We are open to expanding the numbers and are willing to discuss simple, easy USA stick fighting. Doug, Brian, Henry, Antonio and whoever wants to run a tournament applies for sanctioning and so along as they collectively follow these guidelines for tournament structure, safety guidelines, and rule set, we will serve as the board and tournament directors but more so as regional facilitators working as a national collective colleague group with the framework of a board, following simple Robert’s rules of order and organization but with the intent and understanding of no egos, and no one is bigger than the other.

Our organization operates with an open door policy, an open book policy, and open transparency. Minutes from the last meeting are available for review, and funds are easily accessible and visible to all members. While we function as a collective with an ideology-driven approach, we will have an Olympic-style committee framework in place to work towards that path eventually. However, we will not rush the process and will focus on working with people we enjoy collaborating with.

Come join us, have fun, and let's build something great and new while building off 40 years of continued governance and community. 

USA Stick Fighting Collective
