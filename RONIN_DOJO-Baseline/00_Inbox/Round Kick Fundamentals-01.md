Round Kick Fundamentals:
- Three angles:
  - Diagonal up (low diagonal upward)
  - Horizontal (chopping)
  - Diagonal down (downward triangle; strongest but most difficult)

- Three target levels:
  - Leg (e.g., calf, thigh)
  - Body
  - Head

- Angle-to-target combinations (9 total):
  - Leg:
    - Diagonal up (calf shot; keeps space/distance)
    - Horizontal (thigh)
    - Diagonal down
  - Body:
    - Diagonal up
    - Horizontal
    - Diagonal down
  - Head:
    - Diagonal up
    - Horizontal
    - Diagonal down

- Notes on effects/benefits:
  - Calf (diagonal up) shot maintains space and distance to avoid counters (kicks/punches)
  - Horizontal chopping is effective to the thigh
  - Diagonal down is the strongest angle but most difficult

- Practice recommendation:
  - Drill all nine angle/target combinations as a workout for round kick fundamentals
