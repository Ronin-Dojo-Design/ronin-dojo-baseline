# Baseline / Tuff Buffs History Production Board
**Tone:** Perfect Project Plan Dashboard feel | Boulder zen | Kaizen reflective | Planned Passion Produces Purpose | OSSS  
**Brand posture:** Tuff Buffs at CU Boulder with outward-facing Baseline Martial Arts voice  
**Purpose:** Assign the history short-form variants to a dated production calendar with filming notes, asset checklists, and batching workflow.

---

## Production doctrine

This board follows one simple rule:

**Calm beats clever. Repeatable beats heroic.**

That means:
- 2 main history feed posts per week
- supporting Stories on non-feed days
- 1 optional quote card or carousel support asset each week
- one weekly batch session for filming
- one weekly batch session for editing and packaging

---

## Core history series

1. **History Minute**
   - 20–30 second Reel
   - one clear historical truth
   - one takeaway for modern training

2. **Deep Lineage Reel**
   - 40–60 second Reel
   - one lineage/topic explained with more depth

3. **History Carousel**
   - 6–8 slides
   - simple educational sequence
   - highly saveable/shareable

4. **Quote Card / Story Pull**
   - one line of reflective copy
   - one historical or philosophical takeaway
   - easy support content between Reels

# Weekly batch workflow

## Sunday — Planning block — 45 minutes
- choose the week’s 2 main history posts
- choose 2 supporting Story sets
- pull 1–2 relevant clips from vault
- write first draft captions
- decide cover titles

## Monday — Filming block — 45 minutes
Film:
- one history minute
- one deeper lineage reel
- one backup CTA clip

## Tuesday — Editing block — 60 minutes
- cut both Reels
- add captions
- export covers
- prepare first-comment CTAs

## Wednesday — Packaging block — 30 minutes
- build Story sequence
- prepare quote card or carousel
- schedule or queue assets

## Daily — 10 to 15 minutes
- repost to Stories
- respond to comments
- note questions people ask
- save best audience questions for future articles

---

# Filming checklist

## Talking-head setup
- clean background
- quiet room
- chest-up framing
- natural light if possible
- direct eye line
- slower delivery than you think you need

## B-roll checklist
- gloves / mitts
- stick flow
- pad work
- grappling entries
- footwork
- bow / bell / stillness
- team culture moments

## Audio checklist
- check room echo
- record one clean take first
- keep one backup take
- pause between sentences for edit points
