Espada y Daga  
- Waza  
  - form 1, 7, 6a, 11, 12  
  - intro to totsada y largata  
  - 5 count: stick 11, knife palm down, stick 12, knife palm up, retract  
  - 6 count (open): arko, arko, knife palm down, retract  
  - 7 count (closed): arko, knife palm down, arko, knife palm up, retract  

- totsada y largata drill  
  - regular: block 1, stab, backhand arko, stab, stick parry 10, stab, backhand arko, stab  
  - pundo: same as regular, but add footwork to enter into next iteration. Regular, then move left foot into a hook stance, move right foot back into forward stance  
  - extras: block 1, step forward and stab, backhand arko, stab, stick parry 10, stab, backhand arko, stab
