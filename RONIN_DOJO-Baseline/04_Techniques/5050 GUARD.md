# 50/50 GUARD

50/50 Guard  
When we talk about the 50/50 guard, we mean that you’re on your back with one leg across your partner’s hip and the other leg either on the ground or wrapped around their leg. This guard plays a huge role in modern jiu-jitsu and can be a powerful attack and control position.  

The entry into the guard is simple; you’ll begin in a seated position with your partner in front of you. From here, you can either open your guard or maintain your guard on the other side.  

**To maintain the position:**  
- Make sure to keep your partner’s hips pinned down with your leg.  
- Keep your left foot active and ready to attack.  
- You can create more pressure by leaning back and getting an angle.  

In terms of submissions, there’s a huge variety of techniques from this position because the opponent can’t really escape the position without being in danger.  
Make sure to test your entries and see how deep you can get into the 50/50 guard in learning how to see it.  

**Teaching Concepts:**  
- In warm-ups, start by choosing to enter 50/50 because it opens up certain advantages.  
- To see how it takes time to learn how to invest in this position and what works.
