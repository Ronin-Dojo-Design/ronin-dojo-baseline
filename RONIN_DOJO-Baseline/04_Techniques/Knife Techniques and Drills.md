# Knife Techniques and Drills

## Empty Hand Techniques
- kawat only
- 4 count 
- 6 count 
- block clear (intro to hubad)
- parry clear (intro to hubad)
- vining into a lock
- cha-cha double parry
- hubad pagong
- hubad paawas
- block strike strike (set 3 adapted to empty hand)
- block strike strike with additional wide, wide, upper cut, kick, kick

## Knife – Empty Hand
Waza
- 5 basic blocks
  - far hand high
  - close hand high
  - far hand low
  - close hand low
  - far hand forearm low
- block and parry
  - 4 count blocking (block and check)
  - 6 count blocking/parrying (block, check, parry)
  - double block and under hook the upper arm (under hook the forearm on the backhand)
  - double block with inside to body
- disarms and locks
  - palm up high (#1)
  - palm down high (#2)
  - palm up low (#3)
  - palm down low (#4)
- sliding attack
  - far hand, far hand
  - close hand, close hand, clear
  - far hand, block, far forearm, clear (could also do far hand, but this is “untrusted” by many)
  - close hand, far forearm, clear (close hand, close hand block low, parry)
  - double tap: far hand block, close hand check, close hand block low, parry
- guard against retractions

## Flow Drills
- U pattern, with and without disarms
  - entangled arm entrances
    - #1: block and parry into inverted wrist lock, low punch is parried by knife
    - #2: block and parry into inverted wrist lock, low punch is parried by knife
    - #9: parry to dead side, into inverted wrist lock
    - #5: parry to dead side, into wrist lock

## Knife – Knife
Waza
- tapi high on low line (regular and reversed grip)
- tapi high on high line (regular and reversed grip)
- advanced set 1 (tapi high on #9, down/up parry on #10, parry #5)
- advanced set 2 (slice and dice on #9 and #10)
- advanced set 3 (find a few locks on each angle)
- 8 count knife (“Dioni’s 8 count)
