# Technique - Jab

## Teaching cues/talking points
- Extend from the floor up.
- Step in with weight behind shoulder.
- Hit them with your hip then hand.
- Keep the shoulder relaxed.
- Return the hand on the same path.

## Three types of Jab
1. **Straight Jab/Stiff Jab**
2. **Flick Jab/Quick Jab**
3. **Up Jab**
4. Extra credit- vertical jab, slider jab

## Footwork with the Jab
1. **Stationary**
2. **Pull and Drag in all four directions**
3. **Circling with the Jab**

## Targets for the Jab
1. Nose/Chin (straight on/in/out)
2. Side of chin (side step/curl step, 22.5 - 45 degree angle )
3. Jab to the body (usually off side step/curl step to the rear leg side)

## Partner Drills with the Jab
1. Parrying the jab
2. Sidestepping their jab with low to the body jab high to the head jab
3. 3 jab flow- cover/parry/parry and sidestep/sidestep only/head slip only/dip only/bob only/fade only
4. Jab tag/shoulder tag- feint development/timing/rhythm rock
5. Fake high hit low
6. Fake low hit high 
7. Stomp fake/jab step 
8. Front hand down/up jab game 
9. Down parry/return jab return cross return 1-2

## Self defense/Street Fight with the jab
1. Eye jab/eye flick
2. Jab the groin

## 

## Prerequisites
- stable stance
- guard position

## Counters and risks
- can be slipped if telegraphed
- can expose centerline if overextended
