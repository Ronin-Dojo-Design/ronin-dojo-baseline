# Ronin Building Design — Engagement Options

*A Ronin Dojo Design industry practice for builders and contractors.*

Your website should work like your buildings do: engineered to spec, priced before work starts,
and built to hold up. Here are the ways we can work together.

> **All figures on this page are indicative ranges, not a quote.** A formal quote follows a scope
> definition session where we agree, in writing, exactly what is in scope and out of scope.

---

## Option 1 — Fixed-Scope Build

One website, one price, engineered to a locked blueprint.

- **What's included:** scope definition workshop · custom design and build of an agreed page set ·
  mobile-ready, fast-loading, search-engine-ready foundation · launch and handover.
- **How pricing works:** firm fixed price against the locked scope. Any change after scope-lock —
  down to a font color — is a written change order with its own price. No surprises in either
  direction.
- **Indicative range:** **$8,000–$10,000** one-time.
- **Best for:** knowing the full cost before work begins, with a clearly defined site.

## Option 2 — Build + Growth Retainer

The Fixed-Scope Build plus an ongoing engine behind it.

- **What's included:** everything in Option 1, then monthly services you choose from: site care
  (updates, backups, security, fixes) · local search visibility (SEO) · social media management ·
  business automation (lead follow-up, quote tracking).
- **How pricing works:** retainer paid up front each month; work is billed hourly against it at
  our standard rate, with a monthly statement showing exactly where the hours went.
- **Indicative range:** build **$8,000–$10,000** + retainer from **$500–$2,500/month** depending
  on which services you switch on.
- **Best for:** turning the website into a lead source, not just a brochure.

## Option 3 — Time & Materials

Straight hourly work for evolving needs.

- **What's included:** design, development, automation, or consulting work as directed — scoped
  loosely, steered by you month to month.
- **How pricing works:** hourly at **$100–200/hr** depending on the work, invoiced monthly with
  itemized hours. Estimates provided, not guaranteed.
- **Best for:** ongoing improvements, experiments, or work that can't be tightly scoped yet.

## Option 4 — Performance Hybrid (add-on)

We put part of our fee on the line for your results.

- **What's included:** an add-on to Option 2 once lead tracking is in place — a per-qualified-lead
  bonus on top of a base retainer, paid only for leads above an agreed monthly baseline.
- **How pricing works:** we agree in writing, up front, what counts as a *qualified* lead, how
  leads are attributed, and a monthly cap. The bonus is sized against what a converted lead is
  actually worth to your business.
- **Best for:** once we've established a baseline together — proof that our incentives point the
  same direction yours do.

---

### The math that matters

The right question isn't "what does the website cost" — it's **what is a converted lead worth to
you, and what does each customer cost to win?** For a metal-building business, one additional
closed project typically covers years of everything on this page. That's the number we design
around, and it's why we're willing to tie part of our fee to it.
