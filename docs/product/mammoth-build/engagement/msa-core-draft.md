# Master Service Agreement -- core terms

**Parties:** RDD (Ronin Dojo Design), [RDD LEGAL ENTITY TYPE / STATE], located at [RDD ADDRESS]
("RDD") and Mammoth Metal Buildings, [MAMMOTH LEGAL ENTITY TYPE / STATE], located at [MAMMOTH
ADDRESS] ("Company").

**Recital (re-scoped):** RDD is engaged in the business of providing software design and development,
web/marketing, and related agency services and work product; Company wishes to retain RDD to provide
such services and work product as described in one or more Statements of Work (each, an "SOW"),
including the initial SOW at `proposal-sow-draft.md`.

## 1. Term

The Agreement commences on the Effective Date [TBD] and continues until completion of Services under
all outstanding SOWs, unless terminated earlier under Section 6. Each SOW is incorporated into and
governed by this Agreement; where the two conflict, this Agreement controls unless the SOW expressly
states otherwise for that provision.

## 2. Intellectual property

- **Company's ownership of Work Product.** Unless an SOW states otherwise, Company owns the
  Software and Work Product built for it under that SOW, and RDD assigns its right, title, and
  interest in that Work Product to Company upon full payment.
- **RDD's retained Background Technology.** RDD retains sole ownership of all tools, frameworks,
  design systems, code libraries, and other technology that RDD created before the engagement, or
  creates during the engagement but outside the scope of a specific SOW, or that has general
  applicability across RDD's business and is not based on Company's confidential information
  ("Background Technology"). To the extent Background Technology is incorporated into Company's Work
  Product, RDD grants Company a worldwide, perpetual, royalty-free, non-exclusive license to use it
  solely as incorporated, so Company can use its Work Product as intended -- RDD keeps the right to
  reuse, license, or sell its own Background Technology elsewhere.
- This mirrors the source template's Background Technology clause (§6.2/6.3); it is not a new term.

## 3. Confidentiality -- pointer

This Agreement's own confidentiality obligations (non-use, non-disclosure, ownership of confidential
information) apply for the duration of the engagement and are the same shape as the mutual NDA the
Parties sign before discovery. **See `nda-draft.md`** for the confidentiality terms governing the
pre-engagement discovery period; that NDA continues to bind information exchanged before the
Effective Date of this Agreement. Nothing in this Agreement narrows the NDA's protections.

## 4. Payment terms (placeholder)

Fees, invoicing cadence, and payment due dates are set per SOW (see `proposal-sow-draft.md` §5,
Pricing Exhibit) -- **no rate, amount, or due date is fixed by this core document.** Late payment
beyond the SOW's stated grace period is a material breach (mirrors the source template's 10-day
non-payment default trigger, itself a placeholder pending review).

## 5. Limitation of liability

Consistent with the source template's shape, not yet reviewed by counsel:

- Neither Party is liable for special, indirect, incidental, punitive, or consequential damages
  (including lost profits) arising from this Agreement or any SOW.
- RDD's total liability under this Agreement, excluding any indemnification obligations, is capped
  at the amounts actually paid by Company to RDD in the twelve (12) months preceding the claim.
- These are placeholder caps pending attorney review -- not a final position.

## 6. Termination

- **Without cause:** either Party may terminate this Agreement or an SOW on thirty (30) days'
  written notice, unless the SOW says otherwise.
- **With cause:** either Party may terminate on thirty (30) days' written notice of an uncured
  material breach (or immediately if the breach is incurable). Non-payment beyond the grace period in
  §4 is a material breach.
- **Effect:** terminating one SOW does not terminate other active SOWs. On termination, Company pays
  for Services/Work Product delivered through the termination date.

## 7. Change-order mechanism

Fixed-price scope under any SOW changes **only** through a written, signed change order -- **any
change, however small, is a change order** (operator doctrine; same rule stated in
`proposal-sow-draft.md` §6, Change Control). This Agreement does not permit informal scope changes by
email, verbal agreement, or course of dealing.
