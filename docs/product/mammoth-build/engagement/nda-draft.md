# Mutual Nondisclosure Agreement

This Nondisclosure Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into as of [DATE -- TBD] between **RDD
(Ronin Dojo Design)**, [RDD LEGAL ENTITY TYPE / STATE] ("RDD"), and **Mammoth Metal Buildings**,
[MAMMOTH LEGAL ENTITY TYPE / STATE] ("Company"). RDD and Company are each a "Party" and together the
"Parties."

## 1. Purpose

The Parties wish to explore a possible software/design engagement (a website, CRM, and related
enablement work). In connection with that discussion, either Party (the "Disclosing Party") may
disclose confidential technical or business information to the other Party (the "Receiving Party").

## 2. Confidential information

"Confidential Information" means information disclosed in any form (written, verbal, electronic,
visual) that is marked confidential/proprietary or would reasonably be understood as such, including
but not limited to pricing, customer/prospect lists, vendor information, business processes, designs,
source code, databases, business plans, marketing plans and strategy, and financial information.

## 3. What is not confidential

Information already public, later made public without breach, already known to the Receiving Party
before disclosure, lawfully received from a third party, or independently developed without reference
to the Disclosing Party's Confidential Information is not Confidential Information under this
Agreement.

## 4. Non-use and non-disclosure

Each Party will use the other's Confidential Information only to evaluate and discuss the possible
engagement, will not disclose it to third parties except employees/representatives who need it for
that purpose, and will not reverse-engineer any prototype, software, or other object embodying the
other Party's Confidential Information.

## 5. No obligation to proceed

Nothing here obligates either Party to proceed with an engagement; either Party may end these
discussions at any time.

## 6. Ownership; no license

The Disclosing Party retains all right, title, and interest in its Confidential Information. Nothing
in this Agreement grants the Receiving Party any license or rights under any patent, trademark, or
copyright of the Disclosing Party beyond what is expressly stated here.

## 7. Term

Each Party's confidentiality obligations survive until the relevant Confidential Information becomes
publicly known through no fault of the Receiving Party. This NDA's protections continue to apply to
pre-engagement disclosures even after a Master Service Agreement is signed (see `msa-core-draft.md`
§3, Confidentiality -- pointer).
