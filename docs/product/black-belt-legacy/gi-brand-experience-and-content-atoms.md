**Status: PROPOSED input, not ratified.** Distilled from the operator's Thoughts-To-Text

## 1. Brand thesis — "the app that feels like a gi"

Comfort/tradition/wear as the design language: gi-weave textures (double-weave areas, hex
pattern, fabric-on-screen), subtle embroidery-style stitching at low opacity, worn-cotton
"sweat-in-battle" aesthetic. Positioning: connect to fighters, mat, discipline, community,
family, self-defense; "Black Belt Legacy, it's more than an app"; "See you on the mats,
knuckle bumps." Tagline candidates recorded in raw.

**Skin roster (v1 candidates):** BBL worn-gi fabric · tatami/wood/bamboo matte · gi/nogi
variants · puzzle-mat red/blue · Fuji-mat background. Skins prototype in the vault "skin
playground" (Obsidian epic OD-B2) and the SAME skins apply to the front-end site + admin
chrome — chrome surfaces must match skin aesthetic.

## 2. Ritual UX — Bow In / Bow Out

Replace "Sign In"/"Sign Out" with **"Bow In"/"Bow Out"**; navigation mirrors the physical
experience of a dojo (walk in → warm greeting); a Sensei-style greeter bot ("What can we help
you find today") routing free-text to pages or a why-you're-here modal; admin version too.
(The repo's own bow-in/bow-out session ritual becomes product UX — full circle.)

**Gate:** Petey-led `/grill-me` + `/grill-with-docs` with the operator on the flow, then Desi
UX/UI framing, before any build. Auth-copy changes touch Better Auth surfaces — thin-slice
first (copy swap) vs deep ritualization is a grill fork. "Brandon" copy persona revival is
part of this lane.

## 3. Partnerships / sponsorships (outreach lane)

- **Fuji mats** — approach for official sponsorship ("official gi/mat of Black Belt Legacy");
  Fuji-mat skin doubles as the pitch artifact.
- **BJJ Fanatics** — affiliate funnel (sell their videos through BBL) + 5–10% member
  discount as a tier benefit.
- Prereq: professional branded **marketing packet** (uses the skins + brand sheet from the
  vault-kit generator).
- Ad-space product: fight promoters advertise events on BBL; outreach to sell placements;
  drip campaigns for BBL signup.

## 4. Content atoms + the "Legacy" series

Atom types: Community Post (dogfeed as Tony/Brian) · Blog Post · Blog Article. Article
engine ideas: member features, Featured Black Belt of the Week, rapid Zoom interviews,
weekly full-length podcast interview, 30-second teaser + 60-second vertical clip derivatives,
event-promotion articles.

**"<Discipline> Legacy" series taxonomy** (per-discipline content verticals, operator-ordered
by discipline range): Fight Card · No Gi · Wrestlers · Judo · Stick Fight · Wing Chun ·
Karate · Kajukenbo · Doce Pares · Boxing · Sport Karate · Kickboxing · Okinawan Kobudo ·
Budokan · Kyoto · Aikido · Enshin · TKD · Kung Fu · Catch Wrestling · Shooto · USA Karate ·
USA Judo · USA Boxing. (Full list verbatim in raw. Maps naturally onto `Post`/blog + the
content-engine queue; a trend-radar sweep — Reddit/YouTube/podcasts — feeds it, tracked as
Obsidian-epic candidate job OD-D7.)
